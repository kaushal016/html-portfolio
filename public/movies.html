Kaushal's all time Favourite movies

Gather 'round as I unveil my favorite movies, each a masterpiece in its own right. These cinematic treasures have managed to strike a chord within my literary soul, leaving an indelible mark on my imagination.

  1. Ms Dhoni - The Untold Story

    This biographical film beautifully delves into the lesser-known aspects of Dhoni's life, providing a compelling narrative that goes beyond the boundaries of sports. With its heartfelt storytelling, inspiring moments, and a stellar cast, "MS Dhoni: The Untold Story" stands as a must-watch for both cricket enthusiasts and those seeking an uplifting human story.

  2. Mission Mangal

    With a stellar ensemble cast led by Akshay Kumar and Vidya Balan, "Mission Mangal" brilliantly captures the triumphs and struggles of the scientists behind India's Mars Orbiter Mission, leaving viewers inspired and proud of human ingenuity.
